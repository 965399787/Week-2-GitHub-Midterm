"Why Programming?"

I'm going to become old, crippled and ultimately die one day.

Therefore, my biggest goal in life is to leave a long lasting impression, the same way my heros left an impression on me

My heroes are not people, but video games. Zelda, Pokemon, and (unfortunately) League of Legends distracted me for years from the fear of growing up.

To be judgemental, hard-working, lacking any appreciation for life and the details which makes our lives worth enjoying.

"So Why don't I just make my job fun?"

This is why I chose programming, to be the bridge between exploring everything I grew up with, understanding the development of future technology, and make some stupid video games along the way.
